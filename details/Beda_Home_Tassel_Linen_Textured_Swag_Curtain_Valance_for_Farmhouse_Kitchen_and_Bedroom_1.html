Elevate Your Home with Rustic Charm and Style

Beda Home's tassel linen textured swag curtain valance brings a unique combination of cozy comfort and sophisticated elegance to any space. Whether you're renovating a rustic farmhouse or looking to enhance a modern interior, these valances are the perfect complement.

Designed for Beauty and Functionality

These farmhouse curtain valances are crafted from a soft linen blend polyester fabric, offering both comfort and a standout, textured appearance. The tassel detailing adds a whimsical flair, while the rustic aesthetic seamlessly integrates with barn-style, boho, or vintage home decor.

Effortless Installation and Versatile Use

With a 1.5" header and 2.5" rod pocket, these swag panels are easy to hang on most window rods. Ideal for small openings, they add a dramatic, layered look to windows in kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, and basements.

These curtains are formaldehyde-free, making them safe for families with children. Their breathable fabric allows for natural light filtering, creating an airy, private space without sacrificing style. Plus, the easy care instructions mean you can keep them looking beautiful for years to come.

Create a Welcoming Atmosphere with Every Detail

  • Soft linen blend polyester fabric for comfort and style
